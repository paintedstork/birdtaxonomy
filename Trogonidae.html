  • Harpactes Swainson, 1833 Zoological Illustrations ser.2, 3 p.107
    • 510. Malabar Trogon Harpactes fasciatus - (Pennant 1769)
      Original Description: Trogon fasciatus Indian Zoology p.4 pl.5
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 510a. Harpactes fasciatus malabaricus (Gould 1834) [HB#711]
        Original Description: Trogon Malabaricus Proceedings of the Zoological Society of London Part 2, no.16 p.26 (Malabar coast)
        Distribution: W Ghats south of SW Maharashtra till S Tamil Nadu; limited eastward extension towards the spur hills of Nilgiris and Palani hills.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, ROM, USNM, YPM, TNHM
      • 510b. Harpactes fasciatus legerli Koelz 1939 [HB#710]
        Original Description: Harpactes fasciatus legerli Proceedings of the Biological Society of Washington 52 p.79 (Foot of Mahendragiri, Odisha)
        Distribution: Chota Nagpur plateau, NE Ghats, Satpuras, and possibly disjunctly in Surat Dangs south to about Mumbai.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, AMNH, FMNH, USNM
        Notes: The W and E population of this subspecies are not connected and may potentially represent independent lineages. Reports from CE Ghats (AR2001) not evaluated.
    • 511. Red-headed Trogon Harpactes erythrocephalus - (Gould 1834)
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 511a. Harpactes erythrocephalus erythrocephalus (Gould 1834) [HB#713 & 715]
        Original Description: Trogon erythrocephalus Proceedings of the Zoological Society of London Part 2, no.16 p.25
        Taxonomy: Includes hodgsoni Gould 1838, which is the name given to the Himalayan population. If that name were to be recognised, only the population in Mizoram and Tripura shall belong to the nominate subspecies.
        Distribution: Uttarakhand (historical), N Bihar, Sikkim, N Bengal, Assam, W Arunachal, Nagaland, Meghalaya, Cachar, Manipur, Mizoram, and Tripura.
        Specimens: ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, FMNH, MCZ, ROM, YPM
      • 511b. Harpactes erythrocephalus helenae Mayr 1941 [HB#714]
        Original Description: Harpactes erythrocephalus helenae Ibis p.495
        Taxonomy: Not always recognised and sometimes subsumed under either hodgsoni Gould 1838 of Nepal or the nominate.
        Distribution: E Assam and E Arunachal.
        Specimens: BNHS, USNM, YPM
    • 512. Ward's Trogon Harpactes wardi - (Kinnear 1927) [HB#716]
      Original Description: Pyrotrogon wardi Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 47 p.112
      Distribution: N Bengal and adjoining Sikkim and Arunachal from Eaglenest region in the west till the Upper Noa-Dihing valley in the east as well as in E Nagaland.
      Specimens: ZSI, NHMUK, USNM, YPM
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-I.
      Endemic: Eastern Himalaya.
