    • 300. Long-tailed Skua Stercorarius longicaudus - Vieillot 1819
      Taxonomy: Sometimes treated as monotypic.
      Alternative Names: Long-tailed Jaeger
      IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
      • 300a. Stercorarius longicaudus longicaudus Vieillot 1819
        Original Description: Stercorarius longicaudus Nouveau dictionnaire d'histoire naturelle, appliquée aux arts, à l'agriculture, à l'économie rurale et domestique, à la médecine, etc. Nouvelle édition 32 p.157
        Taxonomy: In the absence of specimens, origins of the regional non-breeding population are uncertain. We tentatively assign them to the nominate subspecies as this is the most likely one (RA2012). However, pallescens Løppenthin 1932), also breeds in NE Siberia and it can also occur in S Asia.
        Distribution: Off-shore SW India as well as stragglers in Bay of Bengal.
        Notes: Migration details not worked out but apparently more likely in spring (Karuthedathu 2019).
    • 301. Arctic Skua Stercorarius parasiticus - (Linnaeus 1758) [HB#448]
      Original Description: Larus parasiticus Systema Naturæ per Regna Tria Naturæ, Secundum Classes, Ordines, Genera, Species, cum Characteribus, Differentiis, Synonymis, Locis. Tomus I. Editio decima, reformata 1 p.136
      Alternative Names: Parasitic Jaeger
      Distribution: Winter: Off-shore waters in the Arabian Sea and sporadically in Bay of Bengal. Sporadic elsewhere and sometimes inland during migration.
      Specimens: CMFRI
      IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
      • 302. Pomarine Skua Stercorarius pomarinus - (Temminck 1815) [HB#447]
        Original Description: Lestris pomarinus Manuel d'ornithologie, ou tableau systématique des oiseaux qui se trouvent en Europe (etc.) ed.1 p.514
        Alternative Names: Pomarine Jaeger
        Distribution: Winter: Off-shore waters in the Arabian Sea, mostly south of Goa as well as off-shore Bay of Bengal on the Tamil Nadu coast.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZMA
        IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
        • 303. Brown Skua Stercorarius antarcticus - (Lesson 1831)
          Original Description: Lestris antarcticus Traité d'Ornithologie, ou Tableau Méthodique des ordres, sous-ordres, familles, tribus, genres, sous-genres et races d'oiseaux livr.8 p.616
          Alternative Names: Antarctic Skua, Southern Skua
          IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
          • 303a. Stercorarius antarcticus lonnbergi (Mathews 1912) [HB#446]
            Original Description: Catharacta antarctica lönnbergi Novitates Zoologicae 18 (3) p.212
            Taxonomy: Subspecies assessment tentative (RA2012).
            Distribution: Vagrant. Reports in (northern) summer of shored birds from Sindhudurg (SW Maharashtra, historical), offshore SW Karnataka, twice from Thiruvananthapuram (one historical and one recent) apart from one from Alappuzha (Kerala) off-shore waters.
            Specimens: BNHS
        • 304. South Polar Skua Stercorarius maccormicki - Saunders 1893 [HB#446a]
          Original Description: Stercorarius maccormicki Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 3 p.12
          Distribution: Vagrant. Definitive reports during (northern) summer from the coasts of Karnataka, Kerala and Tamil Nadu.
          Specimens: BNHS
          IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
