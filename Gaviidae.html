    • 346. Red-throated Diver Gavia stellata (Pontoppidan, 1763) [HB#2]
      Original Description: Colymbus Stellatus Pontoppidan, 1763 Den Danske Atlas eller Konge-Riget Dannemark (etc.) 1 p.621
      Alternative Names: Red-throated Loon
      Distribution: Vagrant. Reported only from Sindhudurg coast (Maharashtra).
      IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
