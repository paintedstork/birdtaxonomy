    • 88. Greater Flamingo Phoenicopterus roseus - Pallas, 1811 [HB#73]
      Original Description: Phoenicopterus roseus Zoographia Rosso-Asiatica, sistens omnium animalium in extenso Imperio Rossico (etc.) 2 p.207
      Type Locality: NA
      Distribution: Breeding: Greater Rann of Kachchh and Bhavnagar (both Gujarat). Non-breeding: Most of the western half of the country from Rajasthan-Gujarat till Tamil Nadu. Straggling to the W coast, nearly absent east of Bihar and Odisha. Subject to widespread nomadism.
