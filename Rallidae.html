            • 199b. Gallinula chloropus orientalis Horsfield 1821 [HB#347a]
              Original Description: Gallinula orientalis Transactions of the Linnean Society of London (1) 13 p.195
              Taxonomy: Several birds in S India also show a similar large frontal shield (compared to the birds in N India) and hence this feature may be clinal (see RA2012).
              Distribution: N, Middle, S and Little Andaman including several islets like Havelock, Neil, Tarmugli, Jolly Buoy, and Rutland Is.
              Specimens: BNHS
              Notes: Only a single specimen from Port Blair identified as this subspecies (Abdulali 1969); but photos indicate this to be the dominant subspecies in the islands (S & Little). Currently, BNHS has three specimens identified as this subspecies from S Andamans (#21902, #23130-31).
