    • 655. Malabar Woodshrike Tephrodornis sylvicola Jerdon, 1839 [HB#1068]
      Original Description: T[ephrodornis] Sylvicola Jerdon, 1839 Madras Journal of Literature and Science 10 no.25 p. 236-237 (Malabar Coast)
      Distribution: W Ghats from about south of SW Maharashtra till the southern tip in Kanyakumari district; east through the spur hills of Nilgiri and Palani hills. Historically in S Gujarat.
      Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, USNM, YPM, TNHM, LKO
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      Endemic: Western Ghats.

          Taxonomy: Includes freta Van Tyne & Koelz 1926 of Kangra distict, Himachal.

        • 657b. Tephrodornis pondicerianus pondicerianus (Gmelin, 1789) [HB#1070]

          Taxonomy: Includes warei Koelz 1939 of Londa, Belagavi, NW Karnataka.
          Distribution: Himachal, Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, E Rajasthan, E and S Gujarat, entire peninsular India, east till E Assam, W Meghalaya, and Tripura.

        • 658a. Hemipus picatus capitalis (Horsfield, 1840) [HB#1064]
          Original Description: Muscicapa? capitalis Horsfield, 1840 Proceedings of the Zoological Society of London Part VII p.157 (Assam)
          Distribution: Himachal (local), Uttarakhand, N Uttar Pradesh, N Bengal, Sikkim, Assam, Arunachal, and all the NE hill states. Stragglers to E Rajasthan and Lucknow (Uttar Pradesh).
          Type Specimen: NHMUK#1880.1.1.2938 (Holotype).
          Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, ROM, USNM, YPM
        • 658b. Hemipus picatus picatus (Sykes, 1832) [HB#1065]
          Original Description: Muscicapa picata Sykes, 1832 Proceedings of the Committee of Science and Correspondence of the Zoological Society of London Part 2 no.18 p.85 (Deccan [=Dukhun])
          Distribution: C Indian highlands of Madhya Pradesh, NE Maharashtra, Chhattisgarh, Chota Nagpur plateau of Jharkhand, W Bengal, and Odisha continuing along the NE Ghats of Odisha, Chhattisgarh, and NE Andhra. Semi-disjunctly in W Ghats from Surat Dangs all the way south till the tip of the peninsula; east through SE Ghats reaching the outskirts of Bengaluru (Karnataka) and Vellore (Tamil Nadu).
          Type Specimen: NHMUK#1880.1.1.2938a (Holotype).
          Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, USNM, YPM, MYS
          Notes: Following RA2012, subspecies leggei Whistler 1939 restricted to Sri Lanka (contra AR2001).
