        Original Description: Sula rubripes Gould, 1838 A synopsis of the birds of Australia, and the adjacent islands pt4 App.p.7

      IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 375a. Sula leucogaster plotus (Forster, 1844) [HB#25]
        Original Description: Pelecanus Plotus Forster, 1844 Descriptiones animalium quae in itinere ad maris australis terras per annos 1772, 1773 et 1774 suscepto, collegit, observavit et delineavit (etc.) p.278
        Distribution: Vagrant. Reported from the coasts of Gujarat, Maharashtra, Goa, Karnataka, as well as Lakshadweep.
        Specimens: NHMUK
        Notes: The BNHS specimen (#15034) is a misidentified Masked Booby juvenile. The NHMUK specimen (#1894.6.20.107) from Lakshadweep (vide Hume) has been correctly identified.
