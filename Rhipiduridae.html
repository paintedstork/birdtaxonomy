    • 661. White-throated Fantail Rhipidura albicollis - (Vieillot 1818)
      Alternative Names: White-throated Fantail-flycatcher
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
      • 661a. Rhipidura albicollis canescens (Koelz 1939) [HB#1454]
        Original Description: Leucocirca albicollis canescens Proceedings of the Biological Society of Washington 52 p.68 (Bhadwar, Himachal)
        Distribution: Along the base of W Himalaya from Kashmir, Jammu, Himachal, adjoining Punjab and N Haryana, Uttarakhand, and N Uttar Pradesh.
        Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, FMNH, YPM
      • 661b. Rhipidura albicollis albicollis (Vieillot 1818) [HB#1455]
        Original Description: Platyrhynchos albicollis Nouveau dictionnaire d'histoire naturelle, appliquée aux arts, à l'agriculture, à l'économie rurale et domestique, à la médecine, etc. Nouvelle édition 27 p.13 (Bengal)
        Distribution: Bihar, N Jharkhand, W Bengal, and Sikkim.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, FMNH, YPM
        Notes: Possible migration to lowlands not worked out.
      • 661c. Rhipidura albicollis orissae Ripley 1955 [HB#1457]
        Original Description: Rhipidura albicollis orissae Proceedings of the Biological Society of Washington 68 p.42 (Toda, Bonai, Odisha)
        Taxonomy: See Ripley 1955 for a treatise on this complex. intergrades with albicollis in the plains of W Bengal.
        Distribution: N Chhattisgarh, S Jharkhand, Odisha, and adjoining S W Bengal.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, YPM
      • 661d. Rhipidura albicollis stanleyi Baker 1916 [HB#1456]
        Original Description: Rhipidura albicollis stanleyi Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 36 p.81 (Abor hills, Arunachal)
        Taxonomy: Intergrades with the nominate subspecies in Sikkim. This is the new name for kempi Baker 1913.
        Distribution: Sikkim, Assam, Arunachal, and all NE hill states.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, FMNH, ROM, USNM, YPM
    • 662. Spot-breasted Fantail Rhipidura albogularis - (Lesson 1832)
      Alternative Names: White-spotted Fantail
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      Endemic: Endemic to India.
      • 662a. Rhipidura albogularis albogularis (Lesson 1832) [HB#1458]
        Original Description: Muscicapa (Muscylva) albogularis Voyage aux Indes Orientales par le Nord de l'Europe, les provinces du Caucase, la Géorgie, l'Arménie et la Perse (etc.). Zoologie p.264 (Puducherry [=Pondicherry])
        Taxonomy: Includes pectoralis Jerdon 1847 from Nilgiris. Follows H&M4 in dating it as 1832 (contra 1831), see also Dickinson (2008) and Dickinson et al. (2011).
        Distribution: E Rajasthan (Jaipur, Sawai Madhopur) through Mt. Abu, N and E Gujarat, entire C India including Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, and N and E Maharashtra, along the W Ghats from Surat Dangs till about Goa and N Karnataka; extending through the Deccan transition zone on the eastern slopes of CW Ghats till the base of Nilgiris; NW Telangana, NE Karnataka, CE Ghats of Andhra (south of Godavari river), SE Ghats of Andhra, and Tamil Nadu; sporadic south of the Nilgiris till about Palakkad gap.
        Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, FMNH, HYD
      • 662b. Rhipidura albogularis vernayi (Whistler 1931) [HB#1459]
        Original Description: Leucocirca pectoralis vernayi Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 52 p.40 (Jeypore, S Odisha)
        Taxonomy: Intergrades with R. a. orissae Ripley 1955 in Odisha.
        Distribution: NE Ghats of Andhra and S Odisha.
        Specimens: BNHS, FMNH
    • 663. White-browed Fantail Rhipidura aureola - Lesson 1831
      Alternative Names: White-browed Fantail-flycatcher
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
      • 663a. Rhipidura aureola aureola Lesson 1831 [HB#1451]
        Original Description: Rhipidura aureola Traité d'Ornithologie, ou Tableau Méthodique des ordres, sous-ordres, familles, tribus, genres, sous-genres et races d'oiseaux livr.5 p.390 (Bengal)
        Distribution: N India from about Jammu (sporadic), Himachal, Punjab, and south through Delhi region, west through Rajasthan and Gujarat; east through Gangetic plains till S W Bengal, C India, Chota Nagpur plateau, NE Ghats, NW Ghats from Surat Dangs till about Goa, and N Deccan (locally).
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, ROM, YPM
      • 663b. Rhipidura aureola compressirostris (Blyth 1849) [HB#1452]
        Original Description: Leucocerca compressirostris Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal Vol. 18, Part 2, N° 32 p.815
        Taxonomy: Intergrades with the nominate subspecies in S Maharashtra.
        Distribution: S India in SE Ghats, locally in S and C Deccan till S Maharashtra, eastern edges of CW and SW Ghats, and SW coastal plains (locally).
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, AMNH, FMNH, ROM, USNM, YPM, TNHM, HYD
      • [Rhipidura aureola burmanica (Hume 1880) [HB#1453]]
        Original Description: [Leucocerca] burmanica Stray Feathers Vol. 9, N° 1-3 p.175 (footnote 5)
        Distribution: E Manipur and Mizoram.
        Notes: No recent reports and no specimens (RA2012) as well; but AR2001 has mapped it for Cachar.
