    • 165. Sri Lanka Frogmouth Batrachostomus moniliger Blyth, 1849 [HB#666]
      Original Description: Batrachostomus moniliger Blyth, 1849 Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal 18 p.806
      Taxonomy: Taxonomic status of subspecies roonwali Dutta 2009 from N Karnataka [=N Kanara] and Kodagu [=Coorg] requires re-evaluation as overlapping characteristics exist and too few specimens were examined. Only the IOC accepts this subspecies, and here we treat it as a synonym of the nominate. Sri Lankan population may qualify as a distinct subspecies; in which case the name roonwali would apply to the entire W Ghat population.
      Alternative Names: Ceylon Frogmouth
      Distribution: W Ghats from about Mumbai till the southern tip in Kanyakumari district but absent in the associated hills like Biligirirangans, Servarayan, and Sirumalai.
      Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, TNHM, LKO
      IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-I.
