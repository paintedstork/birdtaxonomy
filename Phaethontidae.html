    • 343. White-tailed Tropicbird Phaethon lepturus - Daudin 1802
      Alternative Names: White Tropicbird
      IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 343a. Phaethon lepturus lepturus Daudin 1802 [HB#19]
        Original Description: Phaëton lepturus Histoire Naturelle par Buffon, Quadrupèdes 14 p.319
        Distribution: Non-breeding: Lakshadweep Sea and off-shore waters thereof. Stragglers elsewhere.
        Notes: All Andaman Sea reports probably of this subspecies only (and not fulvus that breeds in Christmas Is.). There is a specimen in a private collection in Kerala.
      • [Phaethon lepturus fulvus Brandt 1838 ]
        Original Description: Phaëthon fulvus Bulletin Scientifique publié par l'Académie Impériale des Sciences de Saint-Pétersbourg 4 no.7 col.98
        Distribution: SE Bay of Bengal and Andaman Sea.
        Notes: May occur, as they breed southeast of our region in Christmas Is.
    • 344. Red-billed Tropicbird Phaethon aethereus - Linnaeus 1758
      Original Description: Phaëthon aethereus Systema Naturæ per Regna Tria Naturæ, Secundum Classes, Ordines, Genera, Species, cum Characteribus, Differentiis, Synonymis, Locis. Tomus I. Editio decima, reformata 1 p.134
      Alternative Names: Short-tailed Tropicbird
      IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 344a. Phaethon aethereus indicus Hume 1876 [HB#17]
        Original Description: Phaeton indicus Stray Feathers 4 p.481 (Cherbaniani reef, Lakshadweep)
        Distribution: Non-breeding: Off-shore Arabian Sea and sporadically in Bay of Bengal. Stragglers sometimes in the coasts.
        Specimens: BNHS
    • 345. Red-tailed Tropicbird Phaethon rubricauda - Boddaert 1783
      IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 345a. Phaethon rubricauda rubricauda Boddaert 1783 [HB#18]
        Original Description: Phaeton rubricauda Table des planches enluminéez d'histoire naturelle de M. D'Aubenton p.57
        Distribution: Vagrant. Reported from W Bengal and once seen off-shore Great Nicobar Is.
        Specimens: ZRC
        Notes: Bay of Bengal records assumed to be of this subspecies after RA2012.
