    • 1244. Streaked Weaver Ploceus manyar - (Horsfield 1821)
      Original Description: Fringilla manyar Transactions of the Linnean Society of London (1), 13 p.160
      Taxonomy: Western flaviceps intergrades with eastern peguensis in W Bengal.
      Alternative Names: Streaked Weaver Bird
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 1244a. Ploceus manyar flaviceps (Lesson 1831) [HB#1962]
        Original Description: Loxia flaviceps Traité d'Ornithologie, ou Tableau Méthodique des ordres, sous-ordres, familles, tribus, genres, sous-genres et races d'oiseaux p.435 (Pondichery)
        Distribution: Three nearly disjunct population (a) N India in the Indo-Gangetic plains from Jammu, through Punjab, Haryana, Delhi region, adjoining Himachal and Uttarakhand; N Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Jharkhand, and W Bengal (b) Kachchh (connected to Sindh population in Pakistan) and (c) S India from S Maharashtra south till Tamil Nadu and Kerala, extending through E coast of Andhra and Odisha till about W Bengal.
        Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, FMNH, TNHM
      • 1244b. Ploceus manyar peguensis Baker 1925 [HB#1963]
        Original Description: Ploceus manyar peguensis Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 45 p.58
        Distribution: E Bihar, SW and N Bengal, as well as Brahmaputra plains of Assam and adjoining E Arunachal.
        Specimens: BNHS, AMNH, YPM
    • 1245. Baya Weaver Ploceus philippinus - (Linnaeus 1766)
      Alternative Names: Baya, Indian Baya
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 1245a. Ploceus philippinus philippinus (Linnaeus 1766) [HB#1957]
        Original Description: Loxia philippina Systema Naturæ. Editio duodecima reformata. Tomus I [part 1] 12, p.305 (Puducherry)
        Taxonomy: Includes here baya Blyth 1844 from 'India' and sardarpateli Koelz 1952 from Ratnapur, Bhavnagar, Saurashtra, Gujarat. Type locality recently restricted to Puducherry (Oschadleus 2018).
        Distribution: Throughout India from Jammu south till Tamil Nadu, east till Bihar and W Bengal, and west till Rajasthan (excluding Thar) and Gujarat.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, FMNH, MCZ, ROM, USNM, YPM, HYD
      • 1245b. Ploceus philippinus travancoreensis Ali & Whistler 1936 [HB#1958]
        Original Description: Ploceus philippinus travancoreensis Journal of the Bombay Natural History Society 38 p.504 (Kottayam backwaters, Kerala)
        Distribution: SW coast from about Goa through coastal Karnataka and Kerala.
        Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, FMNH, YPM, TNHM
      • 1245c. Ploceus philippinus burmanicus Ticehurst 1932 [HB#1959]
        Original Description: Ploceus infortunatus burmanicus Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 52 p.105
        Taxonomy: Intergrades with the nominate subspecies in W Bengal.
        Distribution: E India from E Bihar, W Bengal, and the Brahmaputra plains of Assam and adjoining E Arunachal as well as the lowland regions of all NE hill states.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, FMNH, YPM
    • 1246. Finn's Weaver Ploceus megarhynchus - Hume 1869
      Alternative Names: Yellow Weaver, Finn's Baya
      IUCN: Endangered. SoIB: High. WLPA: Schedule-I.
      • 1246a. Ploceus megarhynchus megarhynchus Hume 1869 [HB#1960]
        Original Description: Ploceus megarhynchus Ibis p.356 (Kaladhungi, below Nainital, Uttarakhand)
        Taxonomy: Includes here rutledgii Finn 1899 from Nainital, Uttarakhand.
        Distribution: Historically, the Terai region of Uttarakhand and N Uttar Pradesh; now restricted to Rampur and Bijnor districts in N Uttar Pradesh and Udham Singh Nagar district of S Uttarakhand.
        Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, UMMZ
        Notes: See Bharghava (2017).
      • 1246b. Ploceus megarhynchus salimalii Abdulali 1961 [HB#1960a]
        Original Description: Ploceus megarhynchus salimalii Journal of the Bombay Natural History Society 57 p. 660 (Bhutan Dooars, W Bengal)
        Distribution: Terai of N Bengal (historical) and C Assam plains.
        Specimens: BNHS, UMMZ
        Notes: Historical reports from Kolkata could be escaped individuals (see Bhargava 2017).
    • 1247. Black-breasted Weaver Ploceus benghalensis - (Linnaeus 1758) [HB#1961]
      Original Description: Loxia benghalensis Systema Naturæ per Regna Tria Naturæ, Secundum Classes, Ordines, Genera, Species, cum Characteribus, Differentiis, Synonymis, Locis. Tomus I. Editio decima, reformata 1 p.175 (Bengal)
      Taxonomy: Two forms, black-faced and white-faced, have been argued to be two subspecies (see Pittie et al. 1999, Unnithan 2001, Menon et al. 2022).
      Alternative Names: Bengal Weaver, Black-throated Weaver, Black-throated Weaver Bird
      Distribution: Through Indian subcontinent from Jammu S through all states in the Indo-Gangetic and Brahmaputra plains, E Rajasthan, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, NW Maharashtra; on the E peninsula from Odisha, Chhattisgarh, Telangana, Andhra, extending to N Karnataka; sporadically in Manipur valley; recently further S in Chennai and Bengaluru region and expanding further south to Coimbatore and Madurai.
      Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, USNM, YPM, LKO
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II.
