    • 193. Crested Treeswift Hemiprocne coronata - (Tickell 1833) [HB#709]
      Original Description: Hirundo Coronata Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal 2 p.580 (Jungles of Borabhum and Dholbhum, EC India)
      Taxonomy: Includes dryas Ripley 1950 from Juna Rajpipla, S Gujarat.
      Distribution: E Himachal, Uttarakhand foothills, N Bengal, east till about C Assam, hills of C India from E Rajasthan through Madhya Pradesh extending till NE Ghats; Deccan hillocks; W India from S Gujarat till the southern tip of the peninsula; SE Ghats and associated hills in Tamil Nadu, Karnataka and Andhra. Stragglers occurring in intervening regions.
      Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, YPM, TNHM, HYD, LKO
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-I.
