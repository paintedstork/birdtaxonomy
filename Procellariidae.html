    • 353. Barau's Petrel Pterodroma baraui (Jouanin, 1964)
      Original Description: Bulweria baraui Jouanin, 1964 L'Oiseau et la Revue Française d'Ornithologie 34 p.84
      Distribution: Vagrant. Reported from the 9-degree channel in Lakshadweep Sea.
      IUCN: Endangered. WLPA: Schedule-II.

      • 354. Jouanin's Petrel Bulweria fallax Jouanin, 1955 [HB#13a]
        Original Description: Bulweria fallax Jouanin, 1955 L'Oiseau et la Revue Française d'Ornithologie 25 p.158,159,160
        Alternative Names: Jouanin's Gadfly Petrel
        Distribution: Non-breeding: Lakshadweep and Arabian Sea from about Gujarat south till Kerala. Stragglers in Bay of Bengal coasts and Andaman Sea.
        Specimens: ZRC
        IUCN: Near Threatened. WLPA: Schedule-II.

        • 355. Streaked Shearwater Calonectris leucomelas (Temminck, 1836) [HB#7]
          Original Description: Procellaria leucomelas Temminck, 1836 Nouveau recueil de planches coloriées d'oiseaux, pour servir de suite et de complément aux planches enluminées de Buffon livr.99 pl.587
          Alternative Names: White-fronted Shearwater
          Distribution: Non-breeding: Reported sporadically from off-shore waters off Karnataka, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, and Andaman Is.
          IUCN: Near Threatened. WLPA: Schedule-II.

          • 356. Cory's Shearwater Calonectris borealis (Cory, 1881)
            Original Description: Puffinus borealis Cory, 1881 Bulletin of the Nuttall Ornithological Club 6 p.84
            Taxonomy: Sometimes treated as a subspecies of C diomedea (see eBird/Clements).
            Distribution: Vagrant. Reported only from Thaikadappuram (N Kerala).
            Specimens: ZSIKZ
            IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II.

            • 357. Flesh-footed Shearwater Ardenna carneipes (Gould, 1844) [HB#8]
              Original Description: Puffinus carneipes Gould, 1844 The Annals and Magazine of Natural History including Zoology, Botany, and Geology (1), 13 p.365
              Alternative Names: Pink-footed Shearwater (incl. A. creatopus)
              Distribution: Northern summer (=southern winter): Off-shore in Arabian Sea along with SW coast till about Goa, straggling north to Gujarat, seas around Lakshadweep Is., off-shore Tamil Nadu coasts, and Andaman Sea.
              Specimens: BNHS, CMFRI
              IUCN: Near Threatened. WLPA: Schedule-II.

              • 358. Wedge-tailed Shearwater Ardenna pacifica (Gmelin, 1789)
                Original Description: Procellaria pacifica Gmelin, 1789 Systema Naturae. Editio decima tertia, aucta, reformata. Cura Jo. Frid. Gmelin (etc.). Tomus I. Pars II 1 pt2 p.560
                IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II.
                • 358a. Ardenna pacifica chlororhyncha (Lesson, 1831) [HB#9]
                  Original Description: Puffinus chlororhynchus Lesson, 1831 Traité d'Ornithologie, ou Tableau Méthodique des ordres, sous-ordres, familles, tribus, genres, sous-genres et races d'oiseaux livr.8 p.613
                  Taxonomy: Subspecies sometimes not recognised whence treated as monotypic (IOC).
                  Distribution: Non-breeding: Bay of Bengal, Andaman Sea, off-shore W coast and stragglers elsewhere.
                  Specimens: BNHS, KAUNHM, ZSIAN
                  Notes: Regional birds assumed to be from the Indian ocean breeding population.

              • 359. Sooty Shearwater Ardenna grisea (Gmelin, 1789)
                Original Description: Procellaria grisea Gmelin, 1789 Systema Naturae. Editio decima tertia, aucta, reformata. Cura Jo. Frid. Gmelin (etc.). Tomus I. Pars II 1 pt2 p.564
                Distribution: Spring straggler. Reported from Karnataka.
                IUCN: Near Threatened. WLPA: Recent addition.

                • 360. Short-tailed Shearwater Ardenna tenuirostris (Temminck, 1836) [HB#10]
                  Original Description: Procellaria tenuirostris Temminck, 1836 Nouveau recueil de planches coloriées d'oiseaux, pour servir de suite et de complément aux planches enluminées de Buffon livr.99 textpl.587
                  Alternative Names: Slender-billed Shearwater
                  Distribution: Spring straggler. Reported from Gujarat, West Bengal, and Kerala.
                  IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II.

                  • 361. Tropical Shearwater Puffinus bailloni Bonaparte, 1857

                    • 361a. Puffinus bailloni nicolae Jouanin, 1971 [HB#11]
                      Original Description: Puffinus lherminieri nicolae Jouanin, 1971 L'Oiseau et la Revue Française d'Ornithologie 40, pt3-4 p.306
                      Distribution: Vagrant. Storm-blown to Gujarat and Kerala coasts apart from off-shore Maharashtra (yet unpublished).
