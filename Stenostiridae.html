    • 713. Yellow-bellied Fantail Chelidorhynx hypoxanthus - (Blyth 1843) [HB#1450]
      Original Description: Rhipidura hypoxantha Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal 12 p.935 (Darjeeling)
      Taxonomy: Includes noa Koelz 1939 from Naggar, Kullu, Himachal.
      Alternative Names: Yellow-bellied Fairy-fantail, Yellow-bellied Fantail-flycatcher
      Distribution: Along the Himalaya from Kashmir, through Jammu, Himachal, Uttarakhand, adjoining Punjab and Haryana; N Bengal, Sikkim, Arunachal, Assam, Nagaland, Meghalaya, Cachar, Manipur, and Mizoram.
      Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, LKO
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.

      • 714. Grey-headed Canary-Flycatcher Culicicapa ceylonensis - (Swainson 1820)
        Alternative Names: Grey-headed Flycatcher
        IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
        • 714a. Culicicapa ceylonensis calochrysea Oberholser 1923 [HB#1448]
          Original Description: Culicicapa ceylonensis calochrysea Smithsonian Miscellaneous Collections 76 (6) p.8
          Taxonomy: Includes pallidior Ticehurst 1927 from Shimla, Himachal.
          Distribution: Summer: Kashmir, Himachal, Uttarakhand, N Bengal, Sikkim, Arunachal, E Assam, Nagaland, Meghalaya, Cachar, Manipur; disjunctly in C Indian highlands of E Madhya Pradesh, N Maharashtra and NE Ghats of E Chhattisgarh, SE Odisha, and NE Andhra. Winter: Throughout the N peninsula and NE India; NW Ghats till Goa, Telangana and sporadic further south in SE Karnataka. Absent in the coastal plains of SW India and SE India.
          Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, USNM, YPM, HYD, LKO
        • 714b. Culicicapa ceylonensis ceylonensis (Swainson 1820) [HB#1449]
          Original Description: Platyrhynchus Ceylonensis Zoological Illustrations ser.1, 1 pl.13, text
          Taxonomy: Includes piocephala Swainson 1838, new name for ceylonensis Swainson.
          Distribution: CW Ghats from about Kodachadri, Bababudan hills, and Kudremukh, south till Kalakkad in Tamil Nadu; extending east till Biligirirangans in Karnataka.
          Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, FMNH, MCZ, USNM, YPM, TNHM
