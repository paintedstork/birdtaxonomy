    • 407. Glossy Ibis Plegadis falcinellus (Linnaeus, 1766) [HB#71]
      Original Description: Tantalus Falcinellus Linnaeus, 1766 Systema Naturæ. Editio duodecima reformata. Tomus I [part 1] 12, p.241
      Distribution: Throughout India from Ladakh and Kashmir as well as Brahmaputra plains, Manipur valley and Tripura; sporadic in EC India and have occurred in Lakshadweep and Andaman Is.
      Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, FMNH, ROM, LKO
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.

      • 408. Black-headed Ibis Threskiornis melanocephalus (Latham, 1790) [HB#69]
        Original Description: Tantalus melanocephalus Latham, 1790 Index ornithologicus, sive systema ornithologiæ; complectens avium divisionem in classes, ordines, genera, species, ipsarumque varietates: adjectis synonymis, locis, descriptionibus, &c. 2 p.709 (Puducherry [= Pondicherry])
        Alternative Names: White Ibis, Oriental White Ibis
        Distribution: Throughout India except Kashmir, trans and high Himalaya and most of E Himalaya.
        Type Specimen: MNHN#MO-1992-373 (Lecotype).
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, MNHN, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, TNHM
        IUCN: Near Threatened. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II.

        • 409. Red-naped Ibis Pseudibis papillosa (Temminck, 1824) [HB#70]
          Original Description: Ibis papillosa Temminck, 1824 Nouveau recueil de planches coloriées d'oiseaux, pour servir de suite et de complément aux planches enluminées de Buffon livr.51 pl.304, text (India and Sri Lanka)
          Alternative Names: Indian Black Ibis
          Distribution: Throughout the peninsula from south of the Himalayan foothills till the southern tip and east till W Assam. Only stragglers to SW India.
          Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, UMZC, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, ROM, USNM
          IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II.

              Original Description: Platalea Leucorodia Linnaeus, 1758 Systema Naturæ per Regna Tria Naturæ, Secundum Classes, Ordines, Genera, Species, cum Characteribus, Differentiis, Synonymis, Locis. Tomus I. Editio decima, reformata 1 p.139
