      • 1032a. Regulus regulus himalayensis Bonaparte, 1856
        Original Description: Reg[ulus]. himalayensis Comptes Rendus Hebdomadaires des Séances de l'Académie des Sciences Tome 42, 1er semestre, N°17 p.767
        Type Locality: Kotgarh, Himachal
        Distribution: W Himalaya from Gilgit, Ladakh, Kashmir, Jammu, Himachal, and Uttarakhand.
        Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, ROM, USNM
        Notes: Records of _tristis_ subspecies from Ladakh are fraudulent (RA2012).

      • [Regulus regulus yunnanensis Rippon, 1906]
        Original Description: Regulus yunnanensis Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 19 p.19
        Type Locality: NA
        Distribution: Winter: Walong region (NE Arunachal).
        Notes: Walong population almost certainly this greener-backed subspecies while the birds found in Mayodia and Upper Dibang valley could also belong here, but considered tentatively as _sikkimensis_. A thorough investigation required.
