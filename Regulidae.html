      • 1032a. Regulus regulus himalayensis Bonaparte, 1856 [HB#1629]
        Original Description: Reg[ulus]. himalayensis Bonaparte, 1856 Comptes Rendus Hebdomadaires des Séances de l'Académie des Sciences Tome 42, 1er semestre, N°17 p.767 (Kotgarh, Himachal)
        Taxonomy: Includes salimalii Deignan 1954 from Kotgarh, Himachal.
        Distribution: W Himalaya from Gilgit, Ladakh, Kashmir, Jammu, Himachal, and Uttarakhand.
        Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, ROM, USNM
        Notes: Records of tristis subspecies from Ladakh are fraudulent (RA2012).
      • 1032b. Regulus regulus sikkimensis Meinertzhagen & Meinertzhagen, 1926 [HB#1630]
        Original Description: Regulus regulus sikkimensis Meinertzhagen & Meinertzhagen, 1926 Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 46 p.97 (Sikkim)
        Distribution: N Bengal, Sikkim, and Arunachal till about Mishmi hills.
        Type Specimen: NHMUK#1886.11.1.685 (Holotype).
        Specimens: NHMUK, AMNH
      • [Regulus regulus yunnanensis Rippon, 1906 [HB#1631]]
        Original Description: Regulus yunnanensis Rippon, 1906 Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 19 p.19
        Distribution: Winter: Walong region (NE Arunachal).
        Notes: Walong population almost certainly this greener-backed subspecies while the birds found in Mayodia and Upper Dibang valley could also belong here, but considered tentatively as sikkimensis. A thorough investigation required.
