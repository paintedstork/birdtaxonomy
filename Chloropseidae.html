        • 1242b. Chloropsis aurifrons frontalis (Pelzeln, 1856)
          Original Description: Phyllornis frontalis Sitzungsberichte der Kaiserlichen Akademie der Wissenschaften. Mathematisch-Naturwissenschaftliche Classe 69 (1) p.157, pl. 2, fig. 1
          Type Locality: Krishnapur [=Khelgate], N Karnataka
          Distribution: W Ghats from Surat Dangs and sporadically in adjacent coastal plains as well as transition areas on the Deccan side, south till about Palakkad gap as well as SE and CE Ghats till about Godavari river.
          Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, USNM, YPM, MYS, LKO
        • 1242c. Chloropsis aurifrons insularis Whistler & Kinnear, 1932
          Original Description: Chloropsis aurifrons insularis Journal of the Bombay Natural History Society 35 (4) p.752
          Type Locality: NA
          Distribution: SW Ghats roughly south of Palakkad gap.
          Specimens: NHMUK, NHMO, TNHM
          Notes: Palakkad gap considered tentatively as the barrier between subspecies (Ali 1984) though the species is also present locally in Palakkad gap and their subspecific status is unknown.
