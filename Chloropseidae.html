    • 1240. Blue-winged Leafbird Chloropsis moluccensis - Gray 1831
      Original Description: Chloropsis moluccensis The zoological miscellany Part I p.33-34
      Taxonomy: Previously lumped with extralimital C. cochinchinensis.
      Alternative Names: Gold-mantled Chloropsis (incl. C jerdoni)
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 1240a. Chloropsis moluccensis chlorocephala (Walden 1871) [HB#1108]
        Original Description: Phyllornis chlorocephalus The Annals and Magazine of Natural History including Zoology, Botany, and Geology ser. 4, vol. VII, N° 39 p.241
        Taxonomy: Includes chloreus Koelz 1954 from Hahim Bazar, Assam.
        Distribution: Brahmaputra plains of Assam and adjoining Arunachal, and all NE hill states.
        Specimens: ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH
    • 1241. Jerdon's Leafbird Chloropsis jerdoni - (Blyth 1844) [HB#1107]
      Original Description: Phyllornis jerdoni Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal Vol. 13, Part 1, N°149 p.392 (in text) (Continental India)
      Taxonomy: ZSI#35394 listed as type in ZSI register but the collection year is 1847 and from Medinipur (W Bengal).
      Alternative Names: Jerdon's Chloropsis
      Distribution: Entire peninsular India from E Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, C Uttar Pradesh, Jharkhand, S W Bengal, all the way south till the southern tip including W Ghats, and E Ghats; locally in W and E coastal plains as well as Deccan.
      Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, YPM, TNHM, HYD
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 1242. Golden-fronted Leafbird Chloropsis aurifrons - (Temminck 1829)
        Alternative Names: Gold-fronted Chloropsis, Golden-fronted Chloropsis
        IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II.
        • 1242a. Chloropsis aurifrons aurifrons (Temminck 1829) [HB#1103]
          Original Description: Phyllornis aurifrons Nouveau recueil de planches coloriées d'oiseaux, pour servir de suite et de complément aux planches enluminées de Buffon livr.81 pl.484 (Cachar, Assam)
          Distribution: E Himachal, N Haryana, Uttarakhand, N Uttar Pradesh, N Bengal, Sikkim, Arunachal, Assam, all NE hill states; a nearly disjunct population in EC India from C Madhya Pradesh, NE Maharashtra, Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh, S W Bengal, Odisha, and NE Andhra till about Godavari river.
          Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, USNM, YPM, LKO
        • 1242b. Chloropsis aurifrons frontalis (Pelzeln 1856) [HB#1104]
          Original Description: Phyllornis frontalis Sitzungsberichte der Kaiserlichen Akademie der Wissenschaften. Mathematisch-Naturwissenschaftliche Classe 69 (1) p.157, pl. 2, fig. 1 (Krishnapur [=Khelgate], N Karnataka)
          Taxonomy: Includes davidsoni Baker 1920, a new name for malabaricus Gmelin 1788 from Malabar. See Baidya & Bhagat (2018) for resolution of the type locality; the type specimen apparently in Vienna Museum.
          Distribution: W Ghats from Surat Dangs and sporadically in adjacent coastal plains as well as transition areas on the Deccan side, south till about Palakkad gap as well as SE and CE Ghats till about Godavari river.
          Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, USNM, YPM, MYS, LKO
        • 1242c. Chloropsis aurifrons insularis Whistler & Kinnear 1932 [HB#1105]
          Original Description: Chloropsis aurifrons insularis Journal of the Bombay Natural History Society 35 (4) p.752
          Taxonomy: A poorly distinguished subspecies; not always recognised as SW Ghats birds are rather intermediate in size compared to true insularis from Sri Lanka.
          Distribution: SW Ghats roughly south of Palakkad gap.
          Specimens: NHMUK, NHMO, TNHM
          Notes: Palakkad gap considered tentatively as the barrier between subspecies (Ali 1984) though the species is also present locally in Palakkad gap and their subspecific status is unknown.
