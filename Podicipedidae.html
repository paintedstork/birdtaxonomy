    • 92. Red-necked Grebe Podiceps grisegena - (Boddaert, 1783)
      IUCN: Least Concern SoIB: NA WLPA: Schedule-II CITES: NA CMS: Appendix II
      • 92a. Podiceps grisegena grisegena (Boddaert, 1783)
        Original Description: Colymbus grisegena Table des planches enluminéez d'histoire naturelle de M. D'Aubenton p.55
        Type Locality: NA
        Distribution: Vagrant. Reported from Leh (Ladakh), Pong (Himachal), Asan Barrage (Uttarakhand), Bharatpur (Rajasthan), Pariej, Nyari (both Gujarat), and Bhigwan (Maharashtra).
        Notes: No specimens exist. Photo cannot be assessed for subspecies as they differ only in size. On range (Praveen _et al._ 2014), it is conceivable that all records were of the nominate subspecies as that appears to be the closest population. If and when the species is found in E India, possibility of a straggling _P. g. holbollii_ opens up.
