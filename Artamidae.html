    • 653. Ashy Woodswallow Artamus fuscus - Vieillot, 1817 [HB#982]
      Original Description: Artamus fuscus Nouveau dictionnaire d'histoire naturelle, appliquée aux arts, à l'agriculture, à l'économie rurale et domestique, à la médecine, etc. Nouvelle édition 17 p.297
      Alternative Names: Ashy Swallow-shrike
      Type Locality: Bengal
      Distribution: Sub-Himalayan foothills of E Himachal, Uttarakhand and adjoining Punjab and Haryana; N Bengal, Sikkim, Assam, adjoining Arunachal, and all NE hill states. S W Bengal, Chota Nagpur plateau, C Indian highlands, W Gujarat, W and N Maharashtra, Chhattisgarh, Odisha, Andhra, Telangana, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and Kerala. Sporadic in Indo-Gangetic plains, E Rajasthan, WC Madhya Pradesh, Saurashtra, and Deccan.
      Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, ROM, USNM, YPM, TNHM, MYS, HYD, LKO
      IUCN: Least Concern SoIB: Low WLPA: Schedule-II CITES: NA CMS: Appendix II
