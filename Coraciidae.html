        Original Description: Coracias garrulus semenowi Ornithologisches Jahrbuch 13 p.148
        Type Locality: NA
        Distribution: Breeding: Gilgit, Kashmir. Non-breeding: Mostly NW subcontinent in Punjab, Delhi region, Rajasthan, and Gujarat on migration but spilling over to Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, and stragglers further south and east. Also, has occurred in Lakshadweep and Andaman Is.
        Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, USNM, YPM

      • 544a. Coracias benghalensis benghalensis (Linnaeus, 1758)
        Original Description: Corvus benghalensis Systema Naturæ per Regna Tria Naturæ, Secundum Classes, Ordines, Genera, Species, cum Characteribus, Differentiis, Synonymis, Locis. Tomus I. Editio decima, reformata 1 p.106
        Type Locality: Bengal
        Distribution: Himalayan foothills from about Kashmir further south till about C India and the latitude of Mumbai and east till W Bengal and Sikkim.

      • 544b. Coracias benghalensis indicus Linnaeus, 1766
        Original Description: Coracias indica Systema Naturæ. Editio duodecima reformata. Tomus I [part 1] 12, p.159
        Type Locality: NA
        Distribution: The peninsula south of the latitude of Mumbai, as well as Lakshadweep Is.
        Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, AMNH, FMNH, YPM, TNHM, MYS, HYD
        Notes: Subspecies distribution is rather arbitrary and they intergrade.
    • 545. Indochinese Roller Coracias affinis - Horsfield, 1840 [HB#757]
