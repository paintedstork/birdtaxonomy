      • 225a. Haematopus ostralegus longipes Buturlin, 1910 [HB#360]
        Original Description: Haematopus ostralegus longipes Buturlin, 1910 Ornitologicheskii Vestnik 1 p.36
        Taxonomy: Regional birds considered buturlini Dementiev 1941, which breeds in Turkmenistan and S Kazakhstan. Treated here as a junior synonym.
        Distribution: Winter: W coast from Gujarat till S Kerala; E coast from about Kakinada (Andhra) south till Toothukudi (Tamil Nadu). Rare further north in the coasts of Odisha and W Bengal. Sometimes also inland during migration.
        Specimens: BNHS, TNHM
        Notes: Satellite telemetry studies required to see if birds from the range of true longipes winter in India.
      • [Haematopus ostralegus osculans Swinhoe, 1871 [HB#361]]
        Original Description: Haematopus osculans Swinhoe, 1871 Proceedings of the Zoological Society of London Part 2 p.405
        Distribution: W Bengal.
        Notes: May occur in W Bengal as birds likely to be this subspecies have been found in Sundarbans of Bangladesh (Chowdhury & Melville 2018).
