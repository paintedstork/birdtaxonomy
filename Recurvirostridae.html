    • 222. Black-winged Stilt Himantopus himantopus - (Linnaeus, 1758) [HB#430]
      Original Description: Charadrius Himantopus Systema Naturæ per Regna Tria Naturæ, Secundum Classes, Ordines, Genera, Species, cum Characteribus, Differentiis, Synonymis, Locis. Tomus I. Editio decima, reformata 1 p.151
      Distribution: Throughout peninsular India, including Himalaya where it is found at least during migration, Assam plains of both Brahmaputra and Barak, Manipur valley, Tripura, Lakshadweep, and Andaman Is.
      Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, YPM, TNHM, HYD
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.

      • 223. Pied Avocet Recurvirostra avosetta - Linnaeus, 1758 [HB#432]
        Original Description: Recurvirostra Avosetta Systema Naturæ per Regna Tria Naturæ, Secundum Classes, Ordines, Genera, Species, cum Characteribus, Differentiis, Synonymis, Locis. Tomus I. Editio decima, reformata 1 p.151
        Alternative Names: Avocet
        Distribution: NW and N India from Punjab, Haryana, Rajasthan and Gujarat (breeds in Kachchh), Indo-Gangetic and Brahmaputra plains, and along the W and E coast while straggling throughout the country during migration.
        Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, ROM, YPM, LKO
        IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: High. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
