        • [Antigone antigone sharpii (Blanford, 1895), [HB#324]]
          Original Description: Grus (Antigone) sharpii Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 5 p.7
          Distribution: Historically, E Assam and Manipur valley.
          Notes: No specimens traced to support this claim though Higgins (1934) recounts flocks in Manipur valley. The nearest present-day site of this taxon is c.350km N-NE at Indawgyi Lake in Myanmar. Reports of Cranes from Manipur, this as well as Hooded Crane _G. monacha_ Temminck, 1835 (see Karam & Shaikh 2020), have been difficult to evaluate in the absence of independently verifiable evidence; but may well have been true.
