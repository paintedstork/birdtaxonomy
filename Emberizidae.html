    • 1344. Crested Bunting Emberiza lathami Gray, 1831 [HB#2060]
      Original Description: Emberiza lathami Gray, 1831 The zoological miscellany 1 p.2
      Taxonomy: Includes subcristata Sykes 1832 described from Dukhun [=Deccan].
      Distribution: Along the Himalaya from Kashmir, Jammu, Himachal and Uttarakhand; adjoining Indus plains, N Uttar Pradesh, Delhi region, E Rajasthan, Gujarat, C India till Jharkhand, W Bengal in the east and Maharashtra and Telangana in the south; further east in Sikkim, N Bengal, Arunachal, Assam, Nagaland, Meghalaya, Manipur, Mizoram, and Tripura. Sporadic in Odisha, Karnataka, and Kerala.
      Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, ROM, USNM, YPM, TNHM, LKO
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-II.

      • 1345. Black-headed Bunting Emberiza melanocephala Scopoli, 1769 [HB#2043]

        Distribution: Winter: Mostly W India in Punjab, Delhi region, Rajasthan, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Maharashtra, Telangana, and N Karnataka but also sporadically in Gilgit, Himachal, Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, Jharkhand, W Bengal, E Odisha, Andhra, S Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and Kerala.             • 1347a. Emberiza fucata arcuata Sharpe, 1888 [HB#2055]
              Original Description: Emberiza arcuata Sharpe, 1888 Catalogue of the birds in the British Museum 12 p.494 (Shimla)
              Distribution: W Himalaya from Kashmir, Ladakh, through Jammu, Himachal, and Uttarakhand. Winter straggler further south to about Delhi region.
              Type Specimen: NHMUK#1887.6.1.1689 (Syntype).
              Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, YPM
              Notes: Reports from Rajasthan (Sangha et al. 2016) not considered definitive here vis-a-vis first winter E. stewarti.
            • 1347b. Emberiza fucata kuatunensis La Touche, 1925 [HB#2055a]
              Original Description: Emberiza fucata kuatunensis La Touche, 1925 Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 46 p.23
              Taxonomy: Treated under the nominate subspecies in AR2001.
              Distribution: Winter: SW and N Bengal, Assam, Arunachal, Nagaland, Meghalaya, Cachar, Manipur, and Tripura. Stragglers to SW India.
              Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK
              Notes: Stragglers to SW Karnataka, and presumably Kerala as well, of this subspecies (See Monappa & Kumar 2018).

              • 1358. Yellow-breasted Bunting Emberiza aureola Pallas, 1773
                IUCN: Critically Endangered. SoIB: High. WLPA: Schedule-I. CMS: Appendix I.
                • 1358a. Emberiza aureola aureola Pallas, 1773 [HB#2046]
                  Original Description: Emberiza aureola Pallas, 1773 Reise durch verschiedene Provinzen des Russischen Reichs 2 p.711
                  Distribution: Winter: Haryana (historical), Himachal, W Bengal, and stragglers to W coast (Gujarat, Goa, SW Karnataka, and Kerala).
                  Specimens: BNHS, YPM
                  Notes: As per AR2001, the nominate subspecies is what winters in India while RA2012 could not find any specimens. Birds seen in the western parts of India are presumed to be from this population in Europe. This population has declined drastically in the last few decades and may amount to only 120-600 mature individuals. See Shirihai & Svensson (2018) for a picture labelled as this subspecies from West Bengal, India.
                • 1358b. Emberiza aureola ornata Shulpin, 1928
                  Original Description: Emberiza aureola ornata Shulpin, 1928 Annuaire du Musée Zoologique de l'Académie des Sciences de l'Union des Républiques Sovietiques Socialistes 28 livr.3 p.401
                  Distribution: Winter: Jharkhand, W Bengal, Assam, adjoining Arunachal, Nagaland, Meghalaya, Cachar, Manipur valley, and Tripura; as well as Andaman and also Nicobar Is. (historical).
                  Specimens: NHMUK, YPM
                  Notes: RA2012 identified the male specimens as this subspecies.
