      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: High. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 876a. Hemitesia pallidipes pallidipes (Blanford, 1872), [HB#1474]
        Original Description: Phylloscopus pallidipes Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal 41 p.162 (Sikkim)
        Distribution: Uttarakhand, N Bengal, Sikkim, Arunachal, Assam, Nagaland, Meghalaya, Cachar, Manipur with pockets of population in N Odisha (Similipal) and NE Andhra (Araku).
        Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, UMMZ, FMNH, ROM
      • 876b. Hemitesia pallidipes osmastoni (Hartert, 1908), [HB#1475]
        Original Description: Horeites pallidipes osmastoni Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 21 p.107 (Andaman Is)
        Distribution: Middle, S, and Little Andaman Is.
        Specimens: NHMUK, AMNH
        Notes: Most sight reports from the wetlands of Andamans are hard to evaluate considering the presence of Dusky Warbler in that habitat.
