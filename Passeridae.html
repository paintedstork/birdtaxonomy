    • 1263. House Sparrow Passer domesticus - (Linnaeus 1758)
      Original Description: Fringilla domestica Systema Naturæ per Regna Tria Naturæ, Secundum Classes, Ordines, Genera, Species, cum Characteribus, Differentiis, Synonymis, Locis. Tomus I. Editio decima, reformata 1 p.183
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 1263a. Passer domesticus indicus Jardine & Selby 1831 [HB#1938]
        Original Description: Passer indicus Illustrations of ornithology 2, Addenda, sign. D 2 pl. 118 (Bengaluru)
        Taxonomy: Includes nigricollis Burton 1838 from South India.
        Distribution: Throughout India from Kashmir south till the southern tip of peninsula; west till Gujarat and east till Assam, E Arunachal, and all NE hill states. Introduced in all major islands and islets in Andamans, Car Nicobar Is., Kamorta Is. (C Nicobar group), and Great Nicobar Is.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, ROM, USNM, YPM, TNHM, HYD
      • 1263b. Passer domesticus bactrianus Zarudny & Kudashev 1916 [HB#1939a]
        Original Description: Passer domesticus bactrianus Nasha Okhota p.37
        Distribution: Winter: Rajasthan.
        Specimens: BNHS
        Notes: Both this and parkini winters in Rajasthan and separation almost impossible. At least two birds ringed from Bharatpur recovered in Tajikistan thereby establishing the presence of this subspecies (Editors 1967, Editors 1969, AR2001). BNHS collection also has a specimen with a ring collected in 1967, currently labelled as this subspecies, presumably with an assumption that they are of this subspecies.
      • 1263c. Passer domesticus parkini Whistler 1920 [HB#1939]
        Original Description: Passer domesticus parkini Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 41 p.13 (Srinagar, Cashmere)
        Distribution: Breeding: Gilgit, Kashmir, Ladakh, and Lahaul-Spiti (Himachal). Winter: Extending to Rajasthan.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, ROM, USNM
        Notes: AR2001 includes its range till Sikkim but RA2012 considers this a W Himalayan subspecies.
    • 1264. Spanish Sparrow Passer hispaniolensis - (Temminck 1820)

      • 1264a. Passer hispaniolensis transcaspicus Tschusi zu Schmidhoffen 1902 [HB#1940]

        Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, MCZ
        Notes: See Abhinav (2020).
    • 1265. Sind Sparrow Passer pyrrhonotus - Blyth 1845 [HB#1945]
      Original Description: Passer pyrrhonotus Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal 13 p.946
      Alternative Names: Sind Jungle Sparrow
      Distribution: NW India – from Jammu through Punjab, adjoining Pong in Himachal, Haryana, and Delhi region extending east through N and E Rajasthan including Thar region and has straggled to Gujarat (historical).
      Specimens: BNHS
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      Endemic: Indus Plains.
