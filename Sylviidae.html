  • Curruca Bechstein, 1802 Illustrations of ornithology 2 no.6 textpl.94
    • 901. Barred Warbler Curruca nisoria - (Bechstein, 1792)
      Original Description: Motacilla nisoria Kurzgefasste gemeinnützige Naturgeschichte des In- und Auslandes für Schulen und häuslichen Unterricht 1 p.537,note
      IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
      • 901a. Curruca nisoria merzbacheri (Schalow, 1907), [HB#1564a]
        Original Description: Sylvia nisoria merzbacheri Ornithologische Monatsberichte 15 p.3
        Distribution: A passage migrant through Gilgit and Ladakh.
        Specimens: ZMA
        Notes: One female collected from Kataklik, Ladakh (ZMA.AVES.44108) by J Sillem.
    • 902. Lesser Whitethroat Curruca curruca - (Linnaeus, 1758)
      Original Description: Motacilla Curruca Systema Naturæ per Regna Tria Naturæ, Secundum Classes, Ordines, Genera, Species, cum Characteribus, Differentiis, Synonymis, Locis. Tomus I. Editio decima, reformata 1 p.184
      Alternative Names: Hume’s Whitethroat _S [c.] althaea_, Desert/Small Whitethroat _S [c.] minula_
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
      • 902a. Curruca curruca blythi (Ticehurst & Whistler, 1933), [HB#1567]
        Original Description: Sylvia curruca blythi Ibis 1933 p.556 (Kanpur)
        Distribution: Winter: Throughout the subcontinent from Gilgit and Ladakh (at least on passage), N India, C India, east till W Bengal, west till Rajasthan and Gujarat, and south till N Karnataka but sporadic further S.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH
      • 902b. Curruca curruca minula (Hume, 1873), [HB#1569]
        Original Description: Sylvia minula Stray Feathers 1 p.198
        Distribution: Winter: Rajasthan and Gujarat.
        Specimens: BNHS, UMMZ
      • [Curruca curruca margelanica (Sztolcman, 1898), ]
        Original Description: Sylvia margelanica Bulletin de la Société Impériale des Naturalistes de Moscou 15 p.72
        Distribution: Kachchh.
        Notes: Possible in migration. One of the Kutch specimens from Bhuj (BNHS#5864), originally collected by Salim Ali, putatively identified as this subspecies by Vaurie (see Soman 1964).
      • 902c. Curruca curruca althaea (Hume, 1878), [HB#1570]
        Original Description: Sylvia althaea Stray Feathers 7 p.60 (Kashmir)
        Distribution: Breeding: Gilgit, Kashmir, and Ladakh. Winter: Delhi region, Rajasthan, Gujarat, C India, S India including Deccan, eastern edges of W Ghats, and E Ghats of Andhra and Odisha.
        Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MYS, HYD
    • 903. Eastern Orphean Warbler Curruca crassirostris - (Cretzschmar, 1830)
      Original Description: Sylvia crassirostris Atlas zu der Reise im nördlichen Afrika von Eduard Rüppell. Erste Abtheilung. Zoologie. Vögel ["1826"] hft. 4 p. 49, pl. 33, fig. a
      Alternative Names: [Orphean Warbler]
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
      • 903a. Curruca crassirostris jerdoni Blyth, 1847, [HB#1565]
        Original Description: C[urruca]. Jerdoni Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal Vol. 16, N° 5 p.439 (in text) (S India)
        Distribution: Winter: Delhi region, Rajasthan, Gujarat, C India, W Odisha, Deccan, south till Tamil Nadu; sporadic in Gangetic plains of Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, and S W Bengal, as well as W coastal India. Passage further north including Gilgit.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH
    • 904. Asian Desert Warbler Curruca nana - Hemprich & Ehrenberg, 1833 [HB#1571]
      Original Description: Curruca nana Symbolae physicae seu icones et descriptiones corporum naturalium novorum aut minus cognitorum quae ex itineribus per Libyam Aegyptum Nubiam Dongolam Syrian Arabiam et Habessianiam publico institutis (etc.) sig.cc note5
      Distribution: Winter: Rajasthan and Gujarat. More widely in NW India during migration including Jammu, Himachal, Uttarakhand, Punjab, Delhi region, and NW Maharashtra. Straggler in NW Bengal.
      Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, UMMZ, AMNH, MCZ
      IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
      • 905. Common Whitethroat Curruca communis - (Latham, 1787)
        Original Description: Sylvia communis Supplement to the general synopsis of birds p.287
        Alternative Names: Greater Whitethroat
        IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
        • 905a. Curruca communis icterops (Ménétries, 1832), [HB#1566]
          Original Description: Sylvia icterops Catalogue raisonné des objets de zoologie recueillis dans un voyage au Caucase et jusqu'aux frontières actuelles de la Perse p.34
          Distribution: Passage migrant mainly through Punjab, Rajasthan, and Gujarat but sporadically in Ladakh, Jammu, Delhi region, adjoining Uttar Pradesh, and W Maharashtra.
          Specimens: BNHS, UMMZ
