    • 617. Grey-lored Broadbill Serilophus rubropygius (Hodgson, 1839) [HB#864]
      Original Description: Raya Rubropygia Hodgson, 1839 Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal 8 p.36
      Taxonomy: Previously lumped with extalimital S. lunatus (Gould 1834).
      Alternative Names: Collared Broadbill, Silver-breasted Broadbill (incl. S. lunatus EL)
      Distribution: Extreme eastern end. of N Bengal (at least historical), Assam, adjoining Arunachal, Nagaland, Meghalaya, Manipur, Mizoram, and Tripura.
      Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, ROM, USNM, YPM
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-II.
