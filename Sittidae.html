    • 1034. White-cheeked Nuthatch Sitta leucopsis Gould, 1850 [HB#1832]
      Original Description: Sitta leucopsis Gould, 1850 The birds of Asia 2 pt1 pl.46, text (Interior NW Himalaya)
      Distribution: Gilgit, Kashmir, Jammu, Himachal, and Uttarakhand.
      Type Specimen: NHMUK#1860.4.16.494 (Syntype).
      Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, USNM, YPM
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      Endemic: Western Himalaya.

          • 1036a. Sitta frontalis frontalis Swainson, 1820 [HB#1838]
            Original Description: Sitta frontalis Swainson, 1820 Zoological Illustrations ser.1, 1 pl.2
            Taxonomy: Includes simplex Koelz 1939 from Londa, NW Karnataka.
            Distribution: E Himachal, Uttarakhand, adjoining N Uttar Pradesh, N Bihar (Valmiki), N Bengal, Sikkim, Assam, adjoining Arunachal, Nagaland, Meghalaya, Cachar, Manipur, and Mizoram; disjunctly in the hills of Peninsular India from Chota Nagpur plateau region of Jharkhand, NW Odisha, C Indian highlands, Surat Dangs, W Ghats, E Ghats as well as hillocks of E Deccan in Telangana.

        • 1037. Yunnan Nuthatch Sitta yunnanensis Ogilvie-Grant, 1900
          Original Description: Sitta yunnanensis Ogilvie-Grant, 1900 Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 10 p.37

          IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II.

          • 1038. White-tailed Nuthatch Sitta himalayensis Jardine & Selby, 1835 [HB#1834-1835]
            Original Description: Sitta himalayensis Jardine & Selby, 1835 Illustrations of ornithology 3 pl.144 (Shimla)
            Taxonomy: Includes australis Koelz 1952 from Nagaland (recognised by AR2001) and lushaiensis Koelz 1952 from Mizoram, latter treated differently in RA2012.
            Distribution: Along the Himalaya from Himachal, Uttarakhand, N Bengal, Sikkim, Arunachal, Nagaland, Manipur, and Mizoram.
            Type Specimen: NHMUK#1886.6.24.269 (Syntype).
            Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, ROM, USNM, YPM, LKO
            IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II.

            • 1039. Indian Nuthatch Sitta castanea Lesson, 1830 [HB#1830-1831]
              Original Description: Sitta castanea Lesson, 1830 Traité d'Ornithologie, ou Tableau Méthodique des ordres, sous-ordres, familles, tribus, genres, sous-genres et races d'oiseaux livr.4 p.316 (Bengal)
              Taxonomy: Includes prateri Whistler & Kinnear 1932 from NE Ghats.
              Distribution: Foothills of Himalaya in Himachal, Uttarakhand, Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, E Rajasthan, C Indian highlands of Madhya Pradesh, N and E Maharashtra, Chhattisgarh extending to Jharkhand, N and C Odisha, and N Telangana. Disjunctly in the CW Ghats and SE Ghats from N Karnataka, through Kodagu and E Wayanad, south till Nilgiris, extending east into Biligirirangans, Melagiri, and Servarayan hills; NE Ghats of S Odisha and NE Andhra.
              Specimens: UMMZ, AMNH, MCZ, USNM
              IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-II.

                • 1040a. Sitta cinnamoventris almorae Kinnear & Whistler, 1930 [HB#1827]
                  Original Description: Sitta castaneoventris almorae Kinnear & Whistler, 1930 Bulletin of the British Ornithologists' Club 51 p.27 (valley of Ramganga, between Almora & Pethora)
                  Distribution: W Himalaya from Kashmir, Jammu, Himachal, Uttarakhand, and N Uttar Pradesh.

                  Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, FMNH
                • 1040b. Sitta cinnamoventris koelzi Vaurie, 1950 [HB#1829]
                  Original Description: Sitta europaea koelzi Vaurie, 1950 American Museum Novitates no.1472 p.5 (Patkai hills, Arunachal)
                  Taxonomy: E Manipur birds tend to show features of Burmese Nuthatch S neglecta, and may represent an undescribed subspecies.
                  Distribution: Patkai hills of E Arunachal and E Assam, Nagaland, and N Cachar.
                  Type Specimen: AMNH#683925 (Holotype).
                  Specimens: NHMUK, UMMZ, FMNH, USNM
                  Notes: Range insufficiently known.
                • 1040c. Sitta cinnamoventris cinnamoventris Blyth, 1842 [HB#1828]

                  Distribution: N Bengal, Sikkim, Arunachal, adjoining Assam, Meghalaya, Manipur, Mizoram, and Tripura.
                  Type Specimen: ZSI#35572 (Holotype).
                  Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, ROM, USNM, YPM
