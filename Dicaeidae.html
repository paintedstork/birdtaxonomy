    • 1215. Thick-billed Flowerpecker Dicaeum agile - (Tickell, 1833)
      Alternative Names: [Modest Flowerpecker]
      IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: High. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 1215a. Dicaeum agile agile (Tickell, 1833), [HB#1892]
        Original Description: Fringilla Agilis Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal 2 p.578 (Borabhum and Dolbhum, E India)
        Distribution: Along the base of Himalaya from Kashmir, Jammu through Himachal, Punjab, Haryana, Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, Delhi region, E Rajasthan, C India, Bihar, Jharkhand, W Bengal, C and E Gujarat, south through W Ghats, E Ghats, and Deccan (locally) till the southern tip with the exception of SW and SE coast.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, ROM, USNM, YPM, TNHM, HYD
      • 1215b. Dicaeum agile pallescens (Riley, 1935), [HB#1894]
        Original Description: Piprisoma modesta pallescens Proceedings of the Biological Society of Washington 48 p.148
        Distribution: Historically in Nagaland (Kohima), Meghalaya (Mawrygkneng, Khasi hills), Cachar, Manipur (Karong), and Mizoram.
        Specimens: NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, YPM
        Notes: No recent media reports. An unconfirmed report from Tripura.
    • 1216. Yellow-vented Flowerpecker Dicaeum chrysorrheum - Temminck, 1829
      Original Description: Dicaeum chrysorrheum Nouveau recueil de planches coloriées d'oiseaux, pour servir de suite et de complément aux planches enluminées de Buffon livr.80 pl.478 fig.1
      IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 1216a. Dicaeum chrysorrheum chrysochlore Blyth, 1843, [HB#1895]
        Original Description: Dicaeum chrysochlore Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal 12 p.1009
        Distribution: N Bengal, Assam, adjacent Arunachal, and all NE hill states.
        Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, USNM, YPM
    • 1217. Yellow-bellied Flowerpecker Dicaeum melanozanthum - (Blyth, 1843) [HB#1896]
      Original Description: Pachyglossa melanoxantha Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal 12 p.1010
      Distribution: Almora, Uttarakhand (historical), N Bengal, Sikkim, Arunachal, Assam, Nagaland, Meghalaya, Cachar, Manipur, and Mizoram.
      Specimens: UMMZ, FMNH, ROM
      IUCN: Least Concern. WLPA: Schedule-II.
      • 1218. Pale-billed Flowerpecker Dicaeum erythrorhynchos - (Latham, 1790)
        Alternative Names: Tickell's Flowerpecker
        IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II.
        • 1218a. Dicaeum erythrorhynchos erythrorhynchos (Latham, 1790), [HB#1899]
          Original Description: Certhia erythrorhynchos Index ornithologicus, sive systema ornithologiæ; complectens avium divisionem in classes, ordines, genera, species, ipsarumque varietates: adjectis synonymis, locis, descriptionibus, &c. 1 p.299 (Mumbai)
          Distribution: Along the foothills of Himalaya from Kashmir through Jammu, Himachal, Uttarakhand, and adjoining Haryana and N Uttar Pradesh; more regular from Lucknow onwards through E Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Jharkhand, and W Bengal till extreme NE Bengal; sporadic or locally in W Assam and Meghalaya as well as Tripura. Also, entire peninsular India from Gujarat, C Madhya Pradesh, Jharkhand, and south till the southern tip of the peninsula. Absent elsewhere in NE India.
          Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, USNM, YPM
          Notes: Many reports from NE India require verification due to the presence of other juvenile Flowerpeckers having pale bills.
      • 1219. Nilgiri Flowerpecker Dicaeum concolor - Jerdon, 1840 [HB#1902]
        Original Description: Dicaeum concolor Madras Journal of Literature and Science 11 p.227 (Malabar coast)
        Distribution: W Ghats from about Mumbai south till the southern tip of the Ghats in Kanyakumari district; also locally in W coast, and the transition region in the Deccan; extending east through the hillocks of SE Ghats (Melagiri, Yelagiri, Javadhu, Servarayan, and Kolli hills) sometimes reaching Hubballi, Mysuru, and Bengaluru.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, AMNH, FMNH, USNM, YPM, TNHM
        IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II.
        Endemic: Western Ghats.
        • 1220. Plain Flowerpecker Dicaeum minullum - Swinhoe, 1870
          Original Description: Dicaeum minullum Ibis (2) 6 (22) p.240
          IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II.
          • 1220a. Dicaeum minullum olivaceum Walden, 1875, [HB#1901]
            Original Description: Dicaeum olivaceum The Annals and Magazine of Natural History including Zoology, Botany, and Geology (4), 15 p.401
            Distribution: N Bengal, Sikkim, Assam, Arunachal, and all NE hill states.
            Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, USNM, YPM
            Notes: Presence in Chhattisgarh and NE Andhra suspected (see RA2012).
        • 1221. Andaman Flowerpecker Dicaeum virescens - Hume, 1873 [HB#1903]
          Original Description: Dicaeum virescens Stray Feathers 1 no.6 p.482 (Port Blair, Andaman Is.)
          Distribution: N, Middle, S, and Little Andaman Is. as well as Interview, Outram, Nicholson, Havelock, Neil, Tarmugli, and Rutland Is.
          Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK
          IUCN: Not Recognised. SoIB: Moderate. WLPA: Schedule-II.
          Endemic: Andaman & Nicobar Is.
