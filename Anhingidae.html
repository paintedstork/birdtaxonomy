    • 377. Oriental Darter Anhinga melanogaster Pennant, 1769 [HB#29]
      Original Description: Anhinga melanogaster Pennant, 1769 Indian Zoology p.13 pl.12
      Alternative Names: Snake-bird
      Distribution: Throughout India except Kashmir and trans-Himalaya. Straggles to Andaman Is.
