      • 648. Indian Golden Oriole Oriolus kundoo - Sykes, 1832 [HB#953]
        Original Description: Oriolus Kundoo Proceedings of the Committee of Science and Correspondence of the Zoological Society of London Part 2, p. 87
        Type Locality: Deccan [=Dukhun]
        Distribution: Breeding: Gilgit, Ladakh, and Kashmir along the Himalaya till N Bengal. Also, locally in Indo-Gangetic plains, E Rajasthan, Gujarat, C India, W Bengal, and the peninsula. Non-breeding: Throughout Indian peninsula till the southern tip.
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, YPM, TNHM, HYD, GMC, LKO
        IUCN: Least Concern SoIB: Low WLPA: Schedule-II CITES: NA CMS: Appendix II

          • 649a. Oriolus chinensis diffusus Sharpe, 1877
            Original Description: Oriolus diffusus Catalogue of the birds in the British Museum 22 p.189, p.197-198
            Type Locality: Malabar
            Distribution: Winter: N Bengal, Sikkim, Assam, adjoining Arunachal, all NE hill states, S W Bengal, Jharkhand, E Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Odisha, Andhra, Telangana, E Maharashtra, W Ghats from Surat Dangs till the southern tip in Tamil Nadu, W coastal plains, CE Ghats, SE Ghats, and E coastal plains.
            Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, ROM, LKO
          • 649b. Oriolus chinensis andamanensis Beavan, 1867
            Original Description: Oriolus andamanensis Ibis (2) 3 (11) p.326-327
            Type Locality: South Andamans
            Distribution: N, Middle, S, and Little Andaman Is., including islets of N Reef, Interview, Long, Outram, Inglis, Havelock, Neil, Ross (Netaji), Alexandra, Tarmugli, Boat, Rutland, and N Cinque Is.
            Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, MCZ, ROM, USNM, YPM
          • 649c. Oriolus chinensis macrourus Blyth, 1846
            Original Description: Oriolus macrourus Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal Vol. 15, N° 169 p.46-47
            Type Locality: Nicobar Is
            Distribution: Car Nicobar Is., C Nicobar group (Tillanchong, Teresa, Bompoka, Trinket, Kamorta, Nancowry, and Katchal Is.), and Great Nicobar Is.
            Specimens: BNHS, MCZ, ROM, USNM

        • 651. Black-hooded Oriole Oriolus xanthornus - (Linnaeus, 1758)
          Alternative Names: Black-headed Oriole
          IUCN: Least Concern SoIB: Low WLPA: Schedule-II CITES: NA CMS: Appendix II
          • 651a. Oriolus xanthornus xanthornus (Linnaeus, 1758)
            Original Description: Coracias Xanthornus Systema Naturæ per Regna Tria Naturæ, Secundum Classes, Ordines, Genera, Species, cum Characteribus, Differentiis, Synonymis, Locis. Tomus I. Editio decima, reformata 1 p.108
            Type Locality: Chandannagar, W Bengal
            Distribution: Sub-Himalayan foothills and adjacent plains of E Punjab, SE Himachal, NW Haryana, Delhi region, S Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, S W Bengal, N Bengal, Sikkim, Brahmaputra plains of Assam and adjoining Arunachal, and all NE hill states.
            Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, USNM, YPM, LKO
            Notes: Intergradation zone with _maderaspatanus_ roughly along the Ganga river; details not worked out.
          • 651b. Oriolus xanthornus maderaspatanus Franklin, 1831
            Original Description: Oriolus madaraspatanus Proceedings of the Committee of Science and Correspondence of the Zoological Society of London Part 1 p.118
            Type Locality: Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh
            Distribution: Kathiawar in Gujarat (locally), E Rajasthan and adjoining N Gujarat, C India including Madhya Pradesh, N and E Maharashtra, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand and adjoining S W Bengal, Odisha, Telangana, NE Ghats of Andhra, W Ghats from Surat Dangs south till the southern tip, Deccan transition zone of the Ghats of Maharashtra and Karnataka, W coastal plains, SE and CE Ghats, and sporadic elsewhere in Deccan and E coastal plains.
            Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, AMNH, FMNH, USNM
          • 651c. Oriolus xanthornus reubeni Abdulali, 1977
            Original Description: Oriolus xanthornus reubeni Journal of the Bombay Natural History Society 73 p.395
            Type Locality: Wrightmyo, South Andaman Is.
            Distribution: N, Middle, S, and Little Andaman Is. as well as Havelock Is.
            Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK
            Notes: Not as common as other endemic species and subspecies of Andaman Is. Not reports from many islets and very few reports from Middle Andaman.
