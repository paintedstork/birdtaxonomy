      • 648. Indian Golden Oriole Oriolus kundoo Sykes, 1832 [HB#953]
        Original Description: Oriolus Kundoo Sykes, 1832 Proceedings of the Committee of Science and Correspondence of the Zoological Society of London Part 2, p. 87 (Deccan [=Dukhun])
        Taxonomy: Includes baltistanicus Koelz 1939 from Dagoni, Baltistan and turkestanica Zarudny & Kudaschev 1918.
        Distribution: Breeding: Gilgit, Ladakh, and Kashmir along the Himalaya till N Bengal. Also, locally in Indo-Gangetic plains, E Rajasthan, Gujarat, C India, W Bengal, and the peninsula. Non-breeding: Throughout Indian peninsula till the southern tip.
        Type Specimen: NHMUK#1880.1.1.2115 (Syntype).
        Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, YPM, TNHM, HYD, GMC, LKO
        IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.

        • 649. Black-naped Oriole Oriolus chinensis Linnaeus, 1766
          Original Description: Oriolus chinensis Linnaeus, 1766 Systema Naturæ. Editio duodecima reformata. Tomus I [part 1] p.160
          IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
          • 649a. Oriolus chinensis diffusus Sharpe, 1877 [HB#954]
            Original Description: Oriolus diffusus Sharpe, 1877 Catalogue of the birds in the British Museum 22 p.189, p.197-198 (Malabar)
            Distribution: Winter: N Bengal, Sikkim, Assam, adjoining Arunachal, all NE hill states, S W Bengal, Jharkhand, E Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Odisha, Andhra, Telangana, E Maharashtra, W Ghats from Surat Dangs till the southern tip in Tamil Nadu, W coastal plains, CE Ghats, SE Ghats, and E coastal plains.
            Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, ROM, LKO
          • 649b. Oriolus chinensis andamanensis Beavan, 1867 [HB#956]
            Original Description: Oriolus andamanensis Beavan, 1867 Ibis (2) 3 (11) p.326-327 (South Andamans)
            Distribution: N, Middle, S, and Little Andaman Is., including islets of N Reef, Interview, Long, Outram, Inglis, Havelock, Neil, Ross (Netaji), Alexandra, Tarmugli, Boat, Rutland, and N Cinque Is.
            Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, MCZ, ROM, USNM, YPM
          • 649c. Oriolus chinensis macrourus Blyth, 1846 [HB#957]
            Original Description: Oriolus macrourus Blyth, 1846 Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal Vol. 15, N° 169 p.46-47 (Nicobar Is)
            Distribution: Car Nicobar Is., C Nicobar group (Tillanchong, Teresa, Bompoka, Trinket, Kamorta, Nancowry, and Katchal Is.), and Great Nicobar Is.
            Type Specimen: ZSI#18952 (Syntype).
            Specimens: BNHS, MCZ, ROM, USNM

        • 650. Slender-billed Oriole Oriolus tenuirostris Blyth, 1846
          IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: High. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
          • 650a. Oriolus tenuirostris tenuirostris Blyth, 1846 [HB#955]
            Original Description: Oriolus tenuirostris Blyth, 1846 Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal 15 p.48,370 (Assam)
            Distribution: Summer: W Arunachal, Nagaland, and Manipur but also Sikkim (once). Winter: Also from N Bengal, W Assam, Meghalaya, and Mizoram.
            Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, YPM
            Notes: Summer and winter status need better documentation.

        • 651. Black-hooded Oriole Oriolus xanthornus (Linnaeus, 1758)
          Alternative Names: Black-headed Oriole
          IUCN: Least Concern. SoIB: Low. WLPA: Schedule-II. CMS: Appendix II.
          • 651a. Oriolus xanthornus xanthornus (Linnaeus, 1758) [HB#958]
            Original Description: Coracias Xanthornus Linnaeus, 1758 Systema Naturæ per Regna Tria Naturæ, Secundum Classes, Ordines, Genera, Species, cum Characteribus, Differentiis, Synonymis, Locis. Tomus I. Editio decima, reformata 1 p.108 (Chandannagar, W Bengal)
            Distribution: Sub-Himalayan foothills and adjacent plains of E Punjab, SE Himachal, NW Haryana, Delhi region, S Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, S W Bengal, N Bengal, Sikkim, Brahmaputra plains of Assam and adjoining Arunachal, and all NE hill states.
            Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, UMMZ, AMNH, FMNH, MCZ, USNM, YPM, LKO
            Notes: Intergradation zone with maderaspatanus roughly along the Ganga river; details not worked out.
          • 651b. Oriolus xanthornus maderaspatanus Franklin, 1831 [HB#959]
            Original Description: Oriolus madaraspatanus Franklin, 1831 Proceedings of the Committee of Science and Correspondence of the Zoological Society of London Part 1 p.118 (Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh)
            Distribution: Kathiawar in Gujarat (locally), E Rajasthan and adjoining N Gujarat, C India including Madhya Pradesh, N and E Maharashtra, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand and adjoining S W Bengal, Odisha, Telangana, NE Ghats of Andhra, W Ghats from Surat Dangs south till the southern tip, Deccan transition zone of the Ghats of Maharashtra and Karnataka, W coastal plains, SE and CE Ghats, and sporadic elsewhere in Deccan and E coastal plains.
            Specimens: BNHS, ZSI, NHMUK, AMNH, FMNH, USNM
          • 651c. Oriolus xanthornus reubeni Abdulali, 1977 [HB#960a]
            Original Description: Oriolus xanthornus reubeni Abdulali, 1977 Journal of the Bombay Natural History Society 73 p.395 (Wrightmyo, South Andaman Is.)
            Distribution: N, Middle, S, and Little Andaman Is. as well as Havelock Is.
            Type Specimen: BNHS#22021 (Holotype).
            Specimens: BNHS, NHMUK
            Notes: Not as common as other endemic species and subspecies of Andaman Is. Not reports from many islets and very few reports from Middle Andaman.
