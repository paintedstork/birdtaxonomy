    • 213. Masked Finfoot Heliopais personatus (Gray, 1849) [HB#351]
      Original Description: Podica personata Gray, 1849 Proceedings of the Zoological Society of London Part 1, 6 no.186 p.90 pl.4
      Distribution: Historically known only from a few specimens from Namdapha region in E Arunachal, Upper Assam (Dibrugarh, Tinsukia), and Cachar region.
      Specimens: BNHS, YPM
      IUCN: Critically Endangered. WLPA: Schedule-I.
